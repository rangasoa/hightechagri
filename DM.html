With an objective to increase yield and quality of paddy, quantity of yield we developed an eco-friendly and safe granules called “Mr. Rice” through extensive field testing across the countries.

Advantages:
  • Increases number of tillers and panicles.
  • Increase grain numbers.
  • Improves the efficiency of nutrients.
  • Plants gets strength to encounter soil salinity and alkalinity problems.
  • Improves rice quality and storage freshness
Time of application :

Either at the time of planting.

Dosage and Direction :
  • Use 3 Kg “Mr. Rice” granules for growing one acre of Paddy. It can be mixed with any fertilizers or organic manures and apply to soil.
  • In case of saline, alkaline, acidic and wet land soils apply 6Kg of “Mr. Rice”.

Packing : 3 kg, 6 Kg, 15 Kg, 30 Kg.
